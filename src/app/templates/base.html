Friends Oh My God!

We were rewatching some old Friends episodes at home when I took notice that the phrase "oh my god" comes up a lot in that show. As any reasonable person would, I compiled script data and built a website to prove my point.

This webapp lets you check how often phrases like "oh my god" are said in Friends, Seinfeld, and Sex and the City. You can use the search bar below to compute the frequency of any phrase you'd like.
